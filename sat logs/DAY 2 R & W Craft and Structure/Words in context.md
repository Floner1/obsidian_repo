# How to determine most precise word:
- reinforce text meaning
- reading comprehension important
1. Context
	- read provided context carefully
	- match the word we're looking for with the equivalent idea in the other statement.
2. Connotations:
	- Associations we have with words, eg red = anger
	- is sentence pos? find pos choice, and vice versa

# How to attack
1. Summarize text in own words
	- rephrase main idea in own words
	- prompt will make claim, then expand or restate in different words
2. Identify key word, phrase,idea
	- text provides all needed info
	- correct idea reinforce info
	- often 1 word or phrase in text that has same meaning as answer
3. Select word that matches.
